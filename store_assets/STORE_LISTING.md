## Full Description

```
QiblaTime is a complete Islamic companion built with one goal: serve every
Muslim without ads, without tracking, and without collecting any personal data.
Everything works offline. Your data stays on your device.

━━━ PRAYER TIMES & ADHAN ━━━

Accurate prayer times calculated locally for any location in the world.
Receive adhan notifications with your choice of 8 authentic adhan recordings,
including Adhan Al-Makkah and Adhan Al-Madinah. Customize calculation method,
madhab, and notification sound for each prayer independently.

━━━ QURAN ━━━

Read the full Quran with Arabic text in the Amiri font, transliteration, and
translation side by side. Listen to recitations, bookmark your favourite ayahs,
and share verses as beautiful images or videos.

━━━ 2196 HADITHS IN 11 LANGUAGES ━━━

Explore an extensive hadith library covering the most important collections:
Sahih Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, 40 Hadith Nawawi, and more. Every hadith is
available in 11 languages. Search, bookmark, and share hadiths with a single tap.

━━━ PRAYER GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS ━━━

Step-by-step illustrated guide to Salah — from Wudu to Tasleem. Each position
is shown with a clear illustration and full instructions. Perfect for new Muslims
or anyone who wants to review the correct form.

━━━ DAILY DUAS & ADHKAR ━━━

Morning and evening adhkar, duas after prayer, duas for every occasion —
all with Arabic text, transliteration, and translation. Set daily reminders
so you never miss your morning or evening adhkar.

━━━ 99 NAMES OF ALLAH ━━━

Browse all 99 Names of Allah (Asmaul Husna) with their meanings and
transliteration. Use any name as your tasbih counter.

━━━ QIBLA DIRECTION ━━━

Real-time Qibla compass that works anywhere in the world, even without
an internet connection.

━━━ ISLAMIC CALENDAR ━━━

Full Hijri calendar with automatic conversion to Gregorian dates.
See upcoming Islamic events and important dates at a glance.

━━━ PERIOD MODE ━━━

A discreet mode designed for women. During menstruation, prayer tracking
and certain reminders adjust automatically — with full respect for privacy.

━━━ COMPLETE PRIVACY ━━━

No ads. No analytics. No third-party SDKs that track your behavior.
No account required. No data leaves your device. Ever.

━━━ 11 LANGUAGES ━━━

Arabic · English · Spanish · French · German ·
Dutch · Indonesian · Russian · Italian · Portuguese · Turkish

━━━ WORKS OFFLINE ━━━

Prayer times, Quran, hadiths, duas, and the prayer guide all work without
an internet connection. The Qibla compass only needs your last known location.

━━━ MORE FEATURES ━━━

• Prayer streak tracker and weekly analytics
• Focus mode with proximity sensor (auto-pause screen during sujud)
• Hafiz mode for Quran memorization
• Home screen widget showing the next prayer time
• Custom adhan selector with live preview
• High contrast accessibility mode
• Dark theme optimized for night reading

QiblaTime is free. It will always be free. No premium tier. No hidden features
behind a paywall. Just a clean, honest Islamic app built for the community.

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ
```

> **Character count:** ~2,850 — within the 4,000-character limit.
> Remove the `━━━` lines if the Play Console strips special characters during review.

━━━ CALENDARIO ISLÁMICO ━━━
